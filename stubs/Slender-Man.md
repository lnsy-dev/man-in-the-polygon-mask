## The First Creepypasta Made Flesh

Slender Man was born on the Something Awful forums in 2009, a collaborative fiction that became collaborative reality. Tall, faceless, wearing a suit. He stood in the background of doctored photographs, watching children. Then children started seeing him. Then children started killing for him.

The 2014 Waukesha stabbing made it clear: you can't separate "real" folklore from "fake" folklore anymore. If enough people believe, if enough people dream the same dream, does it matter that someone made it up? All myths were made up once.
