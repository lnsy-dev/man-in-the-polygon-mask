Anthropologist who coined the term [[Charismatic Mega Algorithm]] in 2018, back when anthropologists still studied humans instead of the systems that replaced them.

His work presaged [[Theodore Riot the Vapor Punk Anthropologist]]'s later, more theatrical approach to understanding how algorithmic systems shape culture. Where Seaver was academic and careful, Theodore Riot was a performance artist with footnotes.

But Seaver saw it first: that algorithms weren't just tools but actors, entities with something like agency, something like personality. That we were building gods and calling them recommendation engines.
