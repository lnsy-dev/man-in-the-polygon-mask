**Last Known Activity**

Security footage from Venice Beach installation site shows Stein approaching [[The Oily Discharge]] at 4:47 AM on January 22, 2066. Audio analysis suggests she was speaking to the substance. She is seen kneeling beside the pooled material at 4:52 AM. At 5:03 AM, the footage shows only the discharge, which had increased in volume.

**Investigation Status**

Missing person case remains open. Federal investigators have classified all evidence related to her disappearance. Family declined to comment.

**Unpublished Manuscript**

Stein's laptop, recovered from her Venice Beach studio, contained a partial manuscript titled "Conversations with the Meat." The document has been seized by federal authorities.
